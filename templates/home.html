Welcome to Cotton Disease Predictor

Cotton Disease Predictor is an innovative application designed to assist cotton farmers in diagnosing diseases in their crops effectively.

Our system utilizes advanced machine learning techniques to analyze images of cotton plants and accurately predict any diseases present.

Key features of Cotton Disease Predictor:

  • Engineered with cutting-edge machine learning algorithms for disease prediction.
  • Trained on a comprehensive dataset of over 1500 images, achieving a classification accuracy of 90%.
  • Collaborated with agricultural experts and farmers to ensure practical utility and effectiveness in real-world scenarios.
  • Provides farmers with instant disease diagnosis and recommended actions through a user-friendly web interface.

Experience the future of agriculture with Cotton Disease Predictor - revolutionizing the way cotton diseases are detected and managed.
